Who We Are

Dhaka Handicrafts Limited (DH Ltd.) is in the business of the production and export of handicrafts with a commitment to advancing indigenous and impoverished artisans. The motivation for the business of DH Ltd. is to serve traditional crafts producers. Along with business as the prime focus, its slogan is “producers first”. The organizational philosophy and culture as well as the business practices of DH Ltd. were founded on the welfare of vulnerable and impoverished artisans in rural and remote areas of Bangladesh.

Former EDM Handicrafts (Enfants du Monde- Handicrafts) has been transformed into the name Dhaka Handicrafts Ltd. on August 25, 1999. It was a project, initiated by Enfants du Monde- Bangladesh, a Swiss non-governmental organization having headquarters in Geneva.

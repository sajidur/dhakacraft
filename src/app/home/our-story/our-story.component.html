About Us

Dhaka Handicrafts Limited (DH Ltd.) is in the business of production and export of handicrafts with the commitment of advancing indigenous and impoverished artisans. DH Ltd. is a non-profit handicrafts manufacturing and trading concern. It is a hundred percent export oriented crafts producing business entity registered under the Joint Stock Company Act 1994 as Limited Company by Guarantee. The registration number is C- 38459(417)/99 Dated 29 August 1999.

The motivation for business of DH Ltd. is to serve the traditional crafts producers. Along with business as the prime focus, its slogan is “producers first”. The organizational philosophy and culture as well as the business practices of DH Ltd. were founded on welfare of vulnerable and impoverished artisans in rural and remote areas of Bangladesh. Thus, DH Ltd. extends number of welfare services to the traditional artisans – the producers. DH Ltd. is committed to ensure women economic empowerment and social dignity.

Ever since the inception and in the phases of its progressive changes, DH Ltd. has been a producer-welfare oriented organization. It shall encourage independence and self-reliance for both the organization and its beneficiaries. The organization and the people involved shall foster human rights, gender equity; and value the cultural and tradition of the relevant people on their historical context and future prospects.

Organizational Construct

In the mission to assist people improve their living conditions, the business of DH Ltd. has been based on the active participation of all relevant stakeholders in the design, production and quality control of the produce and services to the extent deemed relevant to DH Ltd. management. DH Ltd. is a non-profit and non political organization, operating as far as possible in a self-reliant manner, with the motivation to serve the producers who are prominently traditional artisans and underprivileged communities in a mode of partnership with least financial stake from producers’ side. The functioning of DH Ltd. abides within the realms of values that establish it as pro-producer business entity.

The core values, as far as business operation is concerned, are:

  • Welfare of Artisans
  • Assurance of quality
  • Fairness
  • Transparency
  • Accountability

Vision of DH Ltd.

To be the leading handicrafts exporter of the country

Mission of DH Ltd.

Collaborate with the impoverished artisans in production of natural resource and recycled material based handicrafts

Fair Trade

DH Ltd. is a Fair Trade Organization. It practices fair trade in its business operation. It maintains an active role in promotion of fair trade among the producers, suppliers and relevant stakeholders irrespective of religion, creed, ethnicity, gender, and origin.

However, compliance to FT principles by DH Ltd. shall be subject to approval of Executive Board; and as well as financial strength, availability or affordability of physical facilities, capacity of staff or ability of building capacity of staff, and free choice of partners who are associated with production of DH Ltd. goods and providing services.

DH Ltd. shall continually put effort for gradual upgrading itself towards acquiring the ability to full compliance of FT Principles.

How do we practice Fair-trade
